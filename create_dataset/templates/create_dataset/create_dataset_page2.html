Low Frequency and High frequency are the frequencies below which and above which, respectively, to filter out of the data. Thus the uses are:

  • Low Frequency < High frequency: band-pass filter
  • Low Frequency > High frequency: band-stop filter
  • Low Frequency is not None and High frequency is None: high-pass filter
  • Low Frequency is None and High frequency is not None: low-pass filter
